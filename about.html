About Kreativ Nexus

Creating spaces where people, knowledge, and opportunity meet

Kreativ Nexus is a non-profit organization based in Stockholm, Sweden, working to enable learning, collaboration, networking, cultural exchange, innovation, and professional growth.

Who We Are

We are a community-focused association that connects individuals, communities, professionals, creators, businesses, and educational initiatives. Our role is to make it easier for people and organizations to discover shared interests, learn together, and create meaningful outcomes.

Why We Exist

Many people have valuable skills, ideas, and ambition but need the right network, learning pathway, or opportunity to move forward. Kreativ Nexus exists to bridge that gap and help transform ideas into action.

Vision

A connected community where capability becomes opportunity.

Mission

To enable learning, collaboration, cultural exchange, innovation, and growth through community initiatives and trusted partnerships.

Core Values

Principles that guide our work

Inclusion

Welcoming diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences.

Learning

Encouraging curiosity, skill development, and lifelong growth.

Creativity

Supporting ideas, expression, and practical innovation.

Collaboration

Building trusted relationships across communities and sectors.

Community Impact

Focusing on outcomes that create positive shared value.

About Kreativ Forge

A community-driven study circle

Kreativ Forge is an established study circle operating in collaboration with Folkuniversitetet. It focuses on digital awareness, technology, communication, creativity, lifelong learning, and personal and professional development.

Kreativ Nexus supports and promotes Kreativ Forge as a practical platform for learning, exchange, and community growth.
